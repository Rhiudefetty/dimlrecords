About DIML Records

DIML Records was founded in 2023 by 6 people, with the goal of building a community of artists chasing their music dreams together — growing and helping each other along the way.

We produce, write, record, and mix our own music, staying hands-on with every part of the process. Our band, KTR, brings together our artists: Kravin, Thimz, RhiudeFetty, 6ixx, and Lauvi — each with their own sound, united under one label.

Behind the scenes, our manager Theophilea keeps everything running, guiding the team and the vision forward.

DIML isn't just a label — it's a family chasing the same dream: making music that lasts.
